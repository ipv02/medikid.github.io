MediKid — Support Information

App Name: MediKid

Developer: Igor Pogiba-Vishnevskii

Platform: iOS (iPhone, iPad)

Distribution Model: Freemium (free with premium subscription)

About the App

MediKid helps parents keep a convenient diary of their child’s health and development. You can add information about growth, weight, milestones, and store important notes.

Features

  • Add a child profile with basic information
  • Track growth history and development
  • View growth charts
  • Save notes and important details

Premium Subscription

  • Add more than one child profile
  • Unlock full access to all current and future features
  • Flexible subscription plans: monthly and yearly (save up to 30%)
  • Payments and subscription management are handled by the App Store

Restore Purchases

If you change your device or reinstall the app, you can restore your subscription using the “Restore Purchases” button on the subscription screen.

Privacy

The app does not collect or store any personal data. All child-related information is kept locally on your device.
